> Ctrl-Alt-Intel is a community of independent threat researchers focused on investigating, tracking, and reporting on cybercrime activity. We believe in exposing threat actors who make operational security mistakes, and we publish research to help defenders better understand adversary infrastructure, tooling, and operations.

> Our work is supported by [Hunt.io](https://hunt.io/), a threat intelligence platform that tracks adversary infrastructure and archives open directories for long-term storage and analysis, helping preserve infrastructure artifacts and OPSEC failures for our investigations.

> Have intelligence to share, or interested in collaborating? Contact us at `contact [@] ctrlaltintel [.] com`.
